About Us

HOMIJA is a professional manufacturer of high-quality silicone products in China. Established in 2016, we began by serving the domestic market and successfully expanded to the global stage in 2018.
We specialize in producing a wide range of food-grade products, including silicone kitchenware, baking tools, cake molds, and household items. All our products meet international standards such as FDA and LFGB.
With strong OEM and ODM capabilities, we are committed to providing our worldwide clients with innovative design, reliable quality, and exceptional service. We look forward to building a successful and lasting partnership with you.
