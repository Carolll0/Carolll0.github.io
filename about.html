About Us

Company History

2016: The Foundation - Rooted in Quality
Founded in Guangzhou, China, establishing our manufacturing base and commencing production to serve the domestic market.

2018: Global Expansion - Bridging Markets
Strategically expanded our focus to overseas markets, beginning our journey as an international supplier.

Present: Trusted Partnership - Innovating Together
Evolved into a trusted ODM/OEM partner for a global clientele, including renowned brands, retailers, and e-commerce leaders. We continue to grow, driven by our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Mission & Vission

To be your trusted manufacturing partner. We deliver high-quality, innovative silicone and plastic products that help our clients succeed globally — through expertise, reliability, and end-to-end service.

To become a world-leading and beloved brand in the silicone industry, committed to excellence, sustainability, and meaningful partnerships.

What services can we provide

From design to sales, every department plays a crucial role in ensuring product quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Design Department

We warmly welcome every partner to share us with their good idea. Our custom silicone product manufacturing services are the perfect choice for your project. A design team with over 8 years of experience, having served leading brands. Provide personalized design solutions. Equipped with standardized production processes, with a lead time of 10-12 days and a conversion rate of 80% from samples to bulk production.

Mold Department

Our mold department focuses on providing customers with one-stop mold solutions, from design to production. With extensive experience and professional equipment, our team is capable of developing and manufacturing molds quickly and efficiently based on customers'product design requirements, ensuring the precision and stability of every mold. High-quality molds reduce rework and material waste while extending their service life.

Purchasing Department

Our purchasing department is responsible for managing the procurement of silicone raw material supplies, serving as a key link to ensure smooth production and consistent product quality. With extensive experience in supply chain management, the team strictly selects and inspects raw materials during the procurement stage to guarantee that every batch entering production meets the required standards.

Production Department

Improve the quality and stability of silicone products and adjust the ideal color. Reach your audience and make a lasting impression with vivid silicone products that will retain their vibrant hue for years on end. According to the production schedule, the raw silicone material will be manufactured into a real product through precise molding, demolding, and post-curing processes.

Printing Logo Department

Print logos for your products on demand to enhance your brand influence, one-stop solution that eliminates the hassle of docking multiple suppliers. For the printing needs of metal, silicone and other materials, completes LOGO printing through silk screen printing, pad printing, spraying and UV printing processes.

QC Department

We perform multiple rigorous tests throughout the manufacturing process to ensure that the quality of the product meets expectations when it leaves the factory. And from the initial design concept to fulfillment, our comprehensive custom packaging solutions help your business reduce costs and maximize return.

Sales Department

Our sales department is dedicated to expanding global markets and providing customers with professional and efficient services. The team is well-versed in international trade processes, quickly understanding customer needs and offering the most suitable products and solutions. We maintain close collaboration across departments—including R&D, procurement, and production—to ensure order accuracy and timely delivery.
